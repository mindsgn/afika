# Pocket Money - Facebook Draft

We are building **Pocket Money** for South Africa.

It is a simple mobile app to send and receive digital dollars (USDC) without the usual crypto complexity.

What the first version includes:
- Quick wallet setup on your phone
- Secure login with password (biometric option)
- Send USDC to an email or wallet address
- Claim money in the app
- Bank transfer request flow (manual processing in early phase)

Why this matters:
- Transfers can be slow and expensive for many people.
- We want a faster, more practical money experience for South African users.

We are currently preparing a **private beta on Sepolia** and looking for early testers.

If you would like to try it and give feedback, comment here or send a message.
