# Pocket Money - LinkedIn Draft

We are building **Pocket Money**, a South Africa-first mobile app for simple USDC payments.

The problem we are solving:
- Money movement still feels slower and more expensive than it should.
- Existing crypto products are often too technical for everyday use.

What MVP v1 does:
- Device-local wallet creation.
- Secure unlock with password and optional biometrics.
- Send USDC to Ethereum address or email.
- Claim funds in-app.
- Manual bank transfer request flow for South African users.

Technical direction:
- Expo/React Native app + Go/gomobile core.
- Sepolia private beta first.
- We kept the MVP focused to prioritize reliability, security, and UX quality.

Why this matters in South Africa:
- Faster value movement can help freelancers, small businesses, and families dealing with costly transfer friction.
