## Thread Option A (Launch)

1. South Africa deserves faster money movement. We are building Pocket Money: a simple USDC-first app that feels like Cash App, not a complicated crypto wallet.

2. MVP focus:
- Create wallet on phone
- Secure unlock with password/biometrics
- Send USDC to address or email
- Claim funds in-app

4. We are intentionally shipping MVP v1 without smart accounts first. Reliability and user trust come before advanced infra.
